St. Mary's High School

Jawalakhel, Lalitpur

St. Mary's has emerged as the best educational institution for girls, has earned the respect and honour not only in our country Nepal, but even on an international level. In 1995, At the request of the Higher Secondary Board, Sano Thimi, Sister Eugenia pioneered the Higher Secondary Section of St. Mary’s to give the students an opportunity to continue their studies after their tenth class in the same environment where they felt at home. Thus plus 2 section was established and there were 65 students in the first batch.

Every year about 110 students graduate from three different faculties, Science, Commerce and Humanities. The examinations are conducted by the Higher Secondary Board of Education, Sano Thimi.The Drama Club and Literary Club organized by the students bring out the best in them. Every year the Drama Club oganizes One-Act Play festival and the Literary Club, various Contests and publishes the official Newsletter MARIAN’S ECHO thrice a year.

Apart from a value based education, a homely atmosphere and excellent opportunities are provided to develop their personality.

The promoting team of GCM consists of academicians, researchers, entrepreneurs and professionals in considerably renown both at home and outside. The College has been running +2 (Management), Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) and Master of Business Studies (MBS) programs. The +2 program is affiliated to Higher Secondary Education Board (HSEB), and BBS and MBS programs are affiliated to Tribhuvan University (TU).
